Similar House of Representatives Bills Across Multiple Congresses


A text analysis of all bills introduced in the House of Representatives was performed to determine linguistic similarity of bills from one Congress with the following Congress. Lines are drawn between similar bills, with the lightness of the lines representing the percent similarity.

For each Congress, a text corpus was created from all House of Representatives bills. This text corpus was used to create a Model that could be used compare bills from other Congresses. Next, bills from the previous congress were compared with the newer bills, and if the bills were more than 80% similar, lines are drawn connecting the bills.
